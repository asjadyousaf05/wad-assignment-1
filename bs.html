BS (Computer Science)

Four years undergraduate program at Quaid-e-Azam University

BS (Computer Science) Overview

The BS Computer Science program at QAU offers a solid foundation in computing principles. It equips students with the skills to develop reliable software tools for various domains, blending theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience.

Program Educational Objectives
  • Develop and maintain software using modern tools and technologies.
  • Apply their computing knowledge for societal benefit in diverse areas.
  • Engage in lifelong learning to stay updated with technological advancements.
Program Learning Outcomes
  • Apply computing fundamentals to solve real-world problems.
  • Design and develop solutions to complex computing problems.
  • Work effectively as part of a multidisciplinary team.
  • Engage in lifelong learning and understand professional ethics.
BS Program Structure

The program consists of eight semesters, where students will complete 130 credit hours. The first few semesters focus on foundational courses, and the latter semesters allow specialization in various fields through elective courses. A final-year project consolidates the knowledge gained throughout the program.

Focus Areas
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Data Science
  • Software Engineering
  • Human Centered Computing
  • Information Systems
  • Networks and Security
Courses Overview

The courses are divided into six categories:

  • Computing Core
  • Domain Core
  • Domain Elective
  • Maths and Supporting Courses
  • Elective Supporting Courses
  • General Education Courses
